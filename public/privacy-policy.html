Privacy Policy - Debug-Me

Last updated: April 7, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Debug-Me collects the following information:

  • Account information: Email address and authentication credentials (via Firebase Auth or Google Sign-In)
  • User-generated content: Tasks, schedules, diary entries, expense records, projects, and habits you create within the app
  • Usage data: Daily completion records, focus session logs

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the Debug-Me service
  • To sync your data across devices
  • To generate AI-powered suggestions and analysis (via Google Gemini)
  • To send schedule notifications (with your permission)

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely in Google Firebase Firestore. Each user's data is isolated and protected by Firebase Security Rules. Only you can access your own data.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, database, hosting
  • Google Gemini AI: AI analysis features (data sent to Google's API for processing)

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties, except as necessary to provide the services listed above.

6. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from the app settings.

7. Your Rights

  • Access your data (via the app's Export feature)
  • Delete your account and all data (via Settings > Delete Account)
  • Reset your password (via the login page)

8. Security

We use Firebase Security Rules, HTTPS encryption, and Firebase Authentication to protect your data. API keys are stored securely on the server side.

9. Children's Privacy

Debug-Me is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
